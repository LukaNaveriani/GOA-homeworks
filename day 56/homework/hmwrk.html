Equilibrium

Exploring the Concept of Balance and Stability

What is Equilibrium?

Equilibrium is a state of balance, where all the forces acting on a system are in perfect harmony. It is a fundamental concept in various fields, from physics and chemistry to economics and biology.

Types of Equilibrium

There are different types of equilibrium, including static, dynamic, thermal, and chemical equilibrium. Understanding these types is crucial in various scientific and practical applications.
